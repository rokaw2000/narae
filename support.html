Community connection

Join the naræ+ community.

People can join the naræ+ community to stay connected to the mission, receive updates, and hear about future opportunities as the foundation grows.

What this means

Joining the naræ+ community is a way to stay connected to a mission rooted in healing, cultural belonging, and meaningful support.

It reflects encouragement, connection, and shared purpose, rather than fees, pressure, or formal obligations.

Who it is for

This community is for participants and supporters who feel connected to the heart of naræ+ and want to stay informed as the foundation grows.

It is especially meant for people drawn to healing, cultural connection, encouragement, and supportive community life.

What community members may receive

As naræ+ develops, joining the community may include opportunities such as:

Foundation updates Thoughtful updates about the journey and growth of naræ+.
Future program announcements Notice about future retreats, gatherings, or supportive opportunities.
Community connection A gentle way to stay connected to a mission centered on healing and belonging.
Volunteer opportunities Invitations to future ways of serving, supporting, and participating.
Joining the community does not guarantee access to retreats, services, or programs. Opportunities will be shared as they become available.

Growing with intention

naræ+ is still in its early stages. We want community connection to feel genuine, warm, and sustainable, not rushed.

As the foundation grows, we hope to shape this community carefully so that people feel truly welcomed, respected, and seen.

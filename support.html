Support Naræ+

Support the mission

Support for naræ+ may take different forms, including encouragement, volunteering, partnership, and future financial giving.

Donate

naræ International Foundation is currently in development, and formal donations are not yet active while 501(c)(3) approval is pending.

We look forward to sharing future giving options as the foundation continues to grow.

Volunteer & support

As naræ+ grows, we hope to welcome individuals who want to get involved through service, encouragement, outreach, creativity, cultural knowledge, language skills, or other meaningful forms of support.

Bilingual and multilingual supporters may bring valuable strengths, along with compassion, lived experience, and a desire to help others feel seen and supported.

Ways to stay involved

Supporters may choose to become members, volunteer, share their skills, help amplify the mission, or follow future opportunities to contribute as the foundation develops.

naræ International Foundation has applied for recognition as a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization with the U.S. Internal Revenue Service and is currently pending approval.

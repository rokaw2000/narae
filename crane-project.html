A Project of Hope & Remembrance

The 1000 Crane Project

The 1000 Crane Project is a meaningful initiative of naræ+. It brings together remembrance, encouragement, collective care, and the hope that one day one thousand women may be reached through support, connection, and community.

What it is

A project shaped by intention

The 1000 Crane Project is both symbolic and practical in spirit. It offers a way to hold hope in visible form while reflecting the larger mission of naræ+.

Each crane can stand as a gesture of remembrance, encouragement, support, or quiet solidarity for women whose lives have been changed by illness, treatment, recovery, and uncertainty.

Rather than existing as decoration alone, the project gives form to care and shared intention.

Why one thousand

A number that points forward

At naræ+, one thousand is not only a symbolic number. It also expresses a future hope.

The project reflects the hope that, over time, one thousand women may be supported through encouragement, cultural understanding, community, and meaningful acts of care.

The number gives the project scale, aspiration, and direction.

Remembrance

A crane may honor a life, a struggle, a turning point, a season of treatment, or a story that should not be forgotten.

In that way, the project makes room for memory with tenderness and respect.

Encouragement

A crane can also carry a message of support for someone still moving through fear, exhaustion, change, or healing.

Small in form, it can still communicate presence, care, and hope.

Collective care

Over time, the project may invite participation from individuals, families, schools, communities, and partners who wish to contribute cranes as a shared act of support.

What begins as one folded crane can become part of something larger than any one person.

How it reflects naræ+

Built gradually, with care

The 1000 Crane Project reflects the way naræ+ itself is being built.

Meaningful support is rarely created all at once. It grows gradually through repeated acts of intention, service, generosity, and human connection.

The project honors that slower, steadier kind of building.

A living expression

Small acts that gather into something lasting

Each crane may be small, but the project is not small in meaning.

When repeated over time, simple acts can gather into remembrance, community, and shared purpose. That is part of what this project hopes to embody.

Fold by fold, something lasting begins to take shape.

“Some acts are small enough to hold in the hand, yet large enough to carry memory, encouragement, and hope forward.”
naræ+ guiding spirit

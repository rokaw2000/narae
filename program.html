How We Gather & Support

Program Approach — naræ+

naræ+ is not built for scale. It is built for depth. A small, intentional space for immigrant and culturally diverse women facing breast cancer or gynecologic cancer — to rest, connect, and be truly seen.

🌸
naræ+ Retreat

A space to rest and reconnect

A small, intimate gathering. A chance to step away from treatment and simply be present with women who understand.

Piloting this year. Location and length may vary.

By invitation and trusted connection only.

🪷
naræ+ Circles

Small groups, real connection

Small virtual gatherings built around connection, not medical topics. A shared culture, a creative interest, a language, a season of life.

No expertise needed to lead. Just warmth.

✨
Community Events

Celebration and gathering

Virtual and in-person events for celebration, cultural sharing, and fundraising.

Growing naturally as the community deepens.

The belief behind it all

Healing moves in both directions

Most cancer support is about receiving care. naræ+ believes healing can also move the other way. When a woman is ready, giving back — in any form — can restore something in her too.

That is not an add-on to the mission. It is the mission.

"Care received. Care returned. That is the full circle of naræ+."
naræ+ guiding spirit

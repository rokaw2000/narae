Our mission

naræ International Foundation seeks to uplift immigrant and culturally diverse individuals and communities, especially those impacted by breast and gynecologic cancers, through restorative experiences, cultural education, and meaningful acts of support.

We are beginning intentionally, with small gatherings, trusted relationships, and thoughtful steps that can grow into deeper community care over time.

In Korean, naræ means wings. The name reflects uplift, grace, protection, and the quiet courage it takes to begin again.
Developing nonprofit We are building carefully, with clarity of purpose and room to grow thoughtfully.
Special focus We are especially committed to supporting immigrant and culturally diverse individuals impacted by breast and gynecologic cancers.
How we begin Listening, connection, and community trust come first.

Why naræ+

The crane symbolizes grace, resilience, hope, and the strength to keep moving forward. The red-crowned crane also carries dignity, endurance, and quiet beauty, which closely reflect the spirit of naræ+.

The plus sign represents support, connection, and the belief that even small acts of kindness can help carry someone forward.

Founder’s vision

naræ+ was inspired by the founder’s personal journey as an immigrant and a survivor of breast cancer. For Jihwan, that journey revealed how isolating illness, culture, language, and identity can feel when someone is already carrying so much.

naræ+ grows from a simple belief: healing is not only medical, but also emotional, cultural, and deeply human.

“Like wings, support and community can help lift someone through seasons that feel too heavy to carry alone.”

Jihwan Baek, Founder

What we hope to do

Our work is centered on restoration, cultural belonging, and practical support for people who are too often unseen. We hope to create spaces that feel warm, human, and rooted in dignity.

✿

Restorative experiences

Thoughtful micro-retreats and wellness-centered experiences designed to create room for rest, reflection, encouragement, and renewal.

⟡

Cultural connection

Storytelling, heritage education, and creative expression that help people feel rooted, remembered, and connected to one another.

♡

Community support

Volunteer-centered support that invites participants to give back through service, creating meaningful ways to transform lived experience into encouragement, connection, and hope for others.

Our mission

naræ International Foundation seeks to uplift immigrant and culturally diverse individuals and communities, especially those impacted by breast & gynecologic cancers, through restorative experiences, cultural education, and meaningful acts of support.

Why naræ+

In Korean, naræ means wings. The name reflects the founder’s heritage, inspiration, and personal experience. The crane symbolizes grace, resilience, hope, and the strength to keep moving forward. The red-crowned crane also carries a sense of dignity, endurance, and quiet beauty, which closely reflects the spirit of naræ+. The plus sign in naræ+ represents support, connection, and the belief that even small acts of kindness, service, and shared care can help carry someone forward.

“We believe comfort, dignity, and connection can be healing in their own right. Sometimes even a small space of support can help someone breathe again.”
naræ+ guiding spirit

Founder’s vision

naræ+ was inspired by the founder’s personal journey as an immigrant and a survivor of breast cancer. For Jihwan, that journey revealed how isolating illness, culture, language, and identity can feel when someone is already carrying so much. naræ+ grows from a simple belief: healing is not only medical, but also emotional, cultural, deeply human, and often connected to rediscovering purpose.

A place of understanding

naræ+ hopes to create spaces where immigrants and culturally diverse individuals facing breast and gynecologic cancers can find rest, dignity, encouragement, and a renewed sense of purpose. At its heart, this vision is about making support feel warmer, more personal, and more human. No one should have to navigate illness, identity, and belonging alone. Sometimes the most meaningful help begins with simply being seen, understood, and gently reconnected to community.

“Like wings, support and community can help lift someone through seasons that feel too heavy to carry alone.”
Jihwan Baek, Founder

What we hope to do

Our work is centered on restoration, cultural belonging, and practical support for communities that are too often unseen, including immigrants, culturally diverse individuals, and those navigating breast & gynecologic cancer journeys.

✿

Restorative experiences

Thoughtful micro-retreats and wellness-centered experiences designed to create room for rest, reflection, encouragement, and renewal.

⟡

Cultural connection

Storytelling, heritage education, and creative expression that help people feel rooted, remembered, and connected to one another.

♡

Community support

Volunteer-centered support that turns compassion into practical help for individuals and communities who need it most. We believe giving back can also be part of healing, creating gentle ways for participants to serve, build community, and encourage others. No special expertise is required—only the willingness to care, connect, and show up for one another.

The future we are building

We are beginning with intention: small, thoughtful, and deeply human. Our hope is to grow a lasting network of support shaped by compassion, culture, community, and the belief that healing can also be strengthened through purpose, connection, and giving back.

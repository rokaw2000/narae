Naræ (나래)
pronounced nah-reh
Korean for “wings”

naræ International Foundation is a developing nonprofit supporting immigrant and culturally diverse communities, with special care for those impacted by breast & gynecologic cancers. Through restorative experiences, cultural connection, and meaningful support, we hope to create spaces where people feel seen, supported, and deeply understood.

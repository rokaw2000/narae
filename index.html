Who This Is For

A space for women carrying more than what is visible.

Especially for women from immigrant and culturally diverse communities who may not always feel fully seen in traditional support spaces.

How narae works

How care carries forward

Healing does not move in a straight line. At narae, care begins with support, grows through connection, and for some, returns as care for someone else.

01

Receive Care

Support, understanding, and a space where women do not have to explain every part of their experience.

02

Rebuild Strength

Confidence begins to return through dignity, emotional support, and human connection.

03

Reconnect

Women begin returning to life with greater presence, self-trust, and renewed possibility.

04

Give Forward

Some choose to walk beside someone else, not from obligation, but from understanding.

Founder

Why I created narae

A personal journey that became something larger.

This work was shaped by lived experience, loss, survivorship, and the quiet question of where women like us were in the spaces meant to support healing.

Read more

narae grew from the belief that women can feel more understood, less alone, and over time, stronger in the giving back that healing sometimes makes possible.

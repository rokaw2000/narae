Why naræ+ exists

naræ+ was born from hardship, gratitude, and the conviction that healing is not only about being cared for, but also about rediscovering purpose and offering care to others in return.

What makes it different

naræ+ is shaped by the lived intersection of cancer survivorship, immigrant experience, cultural identity, and public service. It seeks to honor the whole person with warmth, dignity, and meaningful connection.

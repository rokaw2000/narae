Why Be Part

Different roles within the naræ living ecosystem.

naræ is a living ecosystem of care.
It is held by the women at its heart, carried forward by those who have walked the path, and sustained by people and partners who support the work with dignity and intention.

naræ Fellow

A woman at the heart of naræ , receiving care, rest, and community through our programs and retreat experience.

naræ Companion

A naræ Fellow in action, carrying healing forward by walking beside another woman when she is ready. Not separate from the journey, but a continuation of it. A re-folding of her wings.

naræ Steward

A person or organization who helps sustain the spirit and structure of naræ through service, generosity, partnership, advocacy, and care behind the scenes.

About naræ

Why naræ exists.

naræ exists to offer emotionally and culturally grounded care for women whose cancer experience may also be shaped by migration, identity, isolation, language, or the pressure of carrying more than others can see.

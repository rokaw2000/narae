Maryland-based nonprofit

Rooted in dignity, cultural care, and the belief that healing can come full circle.

naræ International Foundation supports immigrant women and culturally diverse women facing breast cancer and gynecologic cancer through dignity, cultural care, encouragement, and meaningful connection.

Shaped by lived experience as an immigrant and two-time breast cancer survivor, naræ+ is rooted in the belief that healing can be strengthened through community, cultural understanding, and purpose, and that giving back, however a woman sees fit, can become part of healing itself.

Why this foundation exists

naræ was born from lived experience, gratitude, cultural identity, and the belief that women deserve to feel seen, supported, and connected with dignity through hardship and healing.

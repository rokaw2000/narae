Leadership & Governance

Leadership

naræ International Foundation is guided by a Board of Directors and an Advisory Board who bring expertise, lived experience, and genuine commitment to this mission.

Our Board of Directors provides governance, oversight, and strategic direction, while our Advisory Board offers specialized guidance, lived insight, and community perspective.

Board of Directors

Governance and oversight

JB
Jihwan Baek
Founder · Executive Director · Board Chair

Public servant, survivor, and founder of naræ.

A lifelong public servant, community leader, and two-time breast cancer survivor, Jihwan founded naræ at the intersection of her lived experiences — immigration, illness, and decades of service to others. Recognized with the Presidential Award through AmeriCorps, she holds an MBA from Frostburg State University and leads naræ with the belief that no woman should face her hardest moments without community.

GT
Genene Tesfaye
Treasurer · Board Member

Financial expert, global development leader, and institution builder.

With more than 23 years in global finance and international development, he has led complex donor-funded programs with precision and integrity across multiple countries. His expertise in budgeting, compliance, and financial analysis provides naræ the institutional backbone needed for responsible growth. He holds an MBA from Cambridge International College in the United Kingdom and is currently pursuing a second MBA at Johns Hopkins University.

SD
Susan E. Dreyer
Secretary · Board Member

Environmental engineer, safety professional, and avid sailor.

A licensed Professional Engineer and Certified Safety Professional, Susan has built a career across higher education, manufacturing, and industry, focusing on environmental compliance, remediation, and human wellbeing. An avid sailor, she brings the same discipline and endurance to her role on the board of naræ. She holds a Master of Science in Environmental Law from the University of Maryland Francis King Carey School of Law.

DC
Daphney N. Cornelius
Board Member

Guardian of dignity, public servant, and generous community presence.

Few people understand human vulnerability as deeply as Daphney. Practiced in walking with others through life's most profound passages, she carries a rare understanding of grief, dignity, and what it truly means to care for a community. Actively engaged in public affairs and known for her generosity of spirit, she grounds naræ's work in the deepest form of human care.

Advisory Board

Guidance and perspective

VB
Vanessa Clark Brooks
Advisory Board Member

Attorney, survivor, and global advocate.

With a J.D. from Georgetown Law and a career spanning international NGOs, global health, and public service — including nearly a decade in Bangladesh and work with UNDP and other UN agencies — Vanessa offers formidable legal counsel and compliance expertise to naræ. A four-time breast cancer survivor, she currently serves as Chief of Staff for a Maryland State Delegate and on the advisory boards of the Tahirih Justice Center and Johns Hopkins Hospital-Bayview.

BN
Bernie Nojadera
Advisory Board Member

Faith leader, community servant, and veteran.

A military veteran and faith leader with deep community roots, Bernie has spent his life in service to others — across cultures, institutions, and generations. That lived commitment to showing up, with compassion and without condition, is exactly the spirit naræ was built to carry forward.

RP
Robin Priestley
Advisory Board Member

Gifted photographer, energy expert, and Navy veteran.

A proud U.S. Navy veteran and Certified Energy Manager with over 25 years delivering sustainability and power quality solutions across industries worldwide, Robin is as comfortable solving multimillion-dollar industrial challenges as he is capturing the human story as a gifted portrait photographer. A skilled speaker and global traveler, he has presented to diverse audiences including first responders — lending to naræ a rare blend of technical expertise, creative vision, and boundless generosity of spirit.

DS
Dami Soh Schlobohm
Advisory Board Member

Musician, cultural bridge, and community builder.

Born in Seoul and raised in Maryland, Dami holds a B.A. in Cello Performance from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County and a Master's in Korean Musicology from Seoul National University. A music educator and session cellist, she teaches cello and Korean traditional music across the DC Metro area. She has served on the boards of the Howard County Arts Council, The Columbia Orchestra, and the Asian Arts and Culture Center — a living example of what it means to bridge worlds, and exactly what naræ stands for.

PT
Philip Tyler
Advisory Board Member

Public servant, community voice, and trust builder.

Shaped by loss and led by heart, Philip is a public servant and active community voice. His rare empathy and genuine warmth ground naræ in its human purpose, while his outside perspective and community connections open doors to people and places the foundation might not otherwise reach. He holds a bachelor's degree from the University of York, United Kingdom.

DW
Daniela Werner
Advisory Board Member

Cross-cultural navigator, global voice, and naræ's bridge to Europe.

Having lived between two countries and navigated life's hardest turns, Daniela understands from the inside what immigrant women carry. Now residing in Germany, she is ideally positioned to help naræ grow beyond U.S. borders — carrying its mission to women who need it most on the other side of the world.

EW
Elizabeth Weglein
Advisory Board Member

Healthcare CEO, community advocate, and legacy builder.

A healthcare leader and community advocate, Elizabeth has spent her career raising standards in care, workforce development, and licensing — serving on state boards and coalitions dedicated to the wellbeing of vulnerable communities. Recognized with the Health Care Heroes Award and appointed by the Governor to Maryland's first Commission on Disabilities, her lifelong work ensuring vulnerable communities receive the care they deserve makes her a natural and powerful ally for naræ's mission.

The leadership of naræ International Foundation will continue to grow thoughtfully alongside the mission it serves.

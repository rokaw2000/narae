Naræ+

Narae International Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering healing, cultural connection, and community for women impacted by breast and gynecologic cancers, especially those whose cultural voices are often underrepresented.

Through small-scale retreats and volunteer engagement, Naræ+ seeks to create spaces for restoration, storytelling, and global connection.

Our story

Learn more about naræ+

naræ+ is a developing nonprofit rooted in healing, culture, and community support. This page shares more about our story, the meaning behind our name, and the kind of care we hope to build over time.

Why naræ+

In Korean, naræ means wings. The name reflects the founder’s heritage and the belief that support can help carry people through difficult seasons.

The crane symbolizes grace, resilience, dignity, and hope. The plus sign represents care, connection, and the quiet power of showing up for one another.

“We believe comfort, dignity, and connection can be healing in their own right. Sometimes even a small space of support can help someone breathe again.”
naræ+ guiding spirit

Founder’s vision

naræ+ was inspired by the founder’s journey as an immigrant and breast cancer survivor. That experience revealed how isolating illness, language, culture, and identity can feel when someone is already carrying so much.

naræ+ grows from a simple belief: healing is not only medical. It is also emotional, cultural, and deeply human.

This vision hopes to create spaces where immigrants and culturally diverse individuals facing breast and gynecologic cancers can find rest, dignity, encouragement, and renewed purpose.

What we hope to do

Our work is centered on restoration, cultural belonging, and practical support for communities that are too often unseen, including immigrants, culturally diverse individuals, and those navigating breast and gynecologic cancer journeys.

We hope to offer thoughtful micro-retreats and wellness-centered experiences that create room for rest, reflection, encouragement, and renewal.

We also hope to encourage cultural storytelling, heritage education, and creative expression that help people feel rooted, remembered, and connected to one another.

Through volunteer-centered support, we believe compassion can become practical help, and that giving back can also be part of healing.

The future we are building

We are beginning with intention: small, thoughtful, and deeply human.

Our hope is to grow a lasting network of support shaped by compassion, culture, community, and the belief that healing can also be strengthened through purpose, connection, and giving back.

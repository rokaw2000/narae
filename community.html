Community, participation, and connection

There is more than one way to be part of naræ+.

naræ+ grows through the people who believe in its purpose. Some receive support. Some volunteer their time. Some collaborate or offer resources. Others simply stay connected to the community.

Every role contributes to a network rooted in dignity, cultural understanding, and meaningful connection.

Who this is for

naræ International Foundation supports immigrant women and culturally diverse women navigating breast cancer and gynecologic cancers through encouragement, cultural care, and community connection.

naræ Fellows

Women who participate in retreats and community-centered support through the foundation.

Volunteers

Individuals who offer time, skills, language support, and operational assistance.

Collaborators

Organizations, artists, healthcare professionals, and community leaders who share the mission.

In-kind support

Professional services, creative contributions, translation, wellness offerings, and other resources strengthen community programs.

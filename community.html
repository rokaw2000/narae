Community & Participation

Ways to Be Part

naræ+ is being shaped with intention and care. Whether you are a woman seeking community or someone who wants to support the mission — there is a place for you.

At the center

The heart of naræ+

naræ+ exists to support immigrant and culturally diverse women facing breast cancer or gynecologic cancer.

Everything surrounding the foundation remains clear about that center. The women are not separate from naræ+. They are its living heart.

A thoughtful foundation

Built with care, not haste

naræ+ is not being built for speed or volume. It is being shaped carefully, with attention to trust, cultural understanding, and the quality of each connection.

The goal is not simply to gather people. It is to build something sincere, steady, and lasting — where each role carries meaning.

🌸 naræ+ Fellows

Women at the center

naræ Fellows are immigrant and culturally diverse women navigating breast cancer or gynecologic cancer. They are the reason naræ+ exists.

When ready, they may also give back — through presence, wisdom, creativity, language, or support for others. Care does not have to end with one person.

🤝 naræ+ Stewards

People who carry the mission

Stewards are people who help care for the mission through their time, presence, skill, and generosity.

They may offer bilingual support, hospitality, outreach, creative help, or quiet work behind the scenes. Their contribution matters deeply.

🌿 Collaborators

Aligned partners

Collaborators may include organizations, artists, healthcare communities, cultural leaders, and practitioners whose values genuinely align with the mission.

The right collaboration broadens what is possible while keeping the work thoughtful and human.

Support

Ways support may take shape

Support is not limited to financial giving. It may also come through donated services, language access, transportation, design, materials, venue support, wellness offerings, cultural programming, or professional guidance.

In-kind support can strengthen the mission in practical and meaningful ways.

Giving

Growing responsibly

naræ International Foundation is a Maryland nonprofit with 501(c)(3) status pending.

Giving opportunities will be shared as the organization continues to grow in a way that is well-structured and ready to steward support with care.

Connect with intention

If the mission of naræ+ resonates with you, you are welcome to introduce yourself, explore collaboration, or share your interest in future involvement.

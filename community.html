Community & Participation

Ways to Be Part of Naræ'

People may connect with naræ' in different ways. Some participate directly as Naræ Fellows. Others remain part of the wider community through encouragement, service, partnership, or support.

Participation

Naræ Fellows come first

Naræ Fellows are the individuals who participate directly in the foundation’s programs and experiences.

The term reflects dignity, partnership, and respect. It is meant to honor people not as passive recipients, but as individuals moving through survivorship, recovery, rebuilding, or renewal with strength and worth.

Naræ Fellows

Fellows take part directly in the foundation’s offerings, including future retreats, circles, and restorative experiences.

Their place in the foundation is central. The work exists to serve people with care, dignity, and meaningful support.

Community Members

Community Members stay connected to the mission and growth of naræ'.

This may include supporters, volunteers, donors, advocates, collaborators, and others who want to remain part of a thoughtful and encouraging community.

Partners & Supporters

Some community members may support the mission through professional skills, cultural insight, partnership, service, or financial generosity.

These contributions help strengthen the foundation as it grows with care and intention.

Approach

Thoughtful by design

naræ' is not being built for scale first. It is being built for depth, quality, and humanity.

The foundation’s offerings are intended to remain thoughtful, personal, and carefully shaped rather than rushed or overly broad.

Growth

Built gradually

Opportunities to participate will unfold over time as the foundation develops its structure, partnerships, and capacity.

This pace is intentional. Like the folded crane and embroidered mark, the work is meant to be shaped carefully.

Future offerings

What participation may include

Micro Retreats Small, carefully designed experiences for rest, reflection, and renewal.
Virtual Circles Online spaces for listening, encouragement, and shared connection.
Cultural & Creative Experiences Future offerings that may draw on culture, creativity, reflection, and mindful restoration.

Community, participation, and thoughtful growth

As naræ+ grows thoughtfully, there may be many ways to take part.

naræ+ is being shaped with care. It is rooted in the belief that meaningful community does not need to grow quickly to grow well. Some may come seeking connection. Some may offer time, skill, cultural insight, partnership, or quiet support.

This is not a large-scale program built for speed. It is a mission growing intentionally, with trust and purpose at its center.

Who this is for

naræ International Foundation exists to support immigrant women and culturally diverse women, including women navigating breast cancer and gynecologic cancers, through encouragement, cultural care, and meaningful community connection.

naræ Fellows

naræ Fellows are women who directly participate in retreats, cultural programs, or community-centered support through the foundation. They are not separate from the mission. They are part of its living heart.

Volunteers

Volunteers may support the work through hospitality, outreach, administration, language support, planning, creative help, or steady acts of care behind the scenes as needs emerge over time.

Collaborators

naræ+ welcomes thoughtful collaboration with organizations, artists, healthcare communities, cultural leaders, and others whose values align with dignity, healing, and meaningful support.

In-kind support

Support does not always arrive financially. In-kind contributions may include professional services, translation, creative work, materials, venue support, wellness offerings, donated goods, or other resources that strengthen the mission with care.

Donations

naræ International Foundation is a Maryland nonprofit organization currently seeking 501(c)(3) recognition. Donation opportunities will be shared in time, as the organization grows with intention and the structure is ready to support them responsibly.

Reach out thoughtfully

If the mission of naræ+ resonates with you, and you would like to introduce yourself, explore collaboration, offer in-kind support, or express interest in future involvement, you are welcome to send a message.

Built with care

naræ+ is being shaped intentionally, one relationship and one thoughtful step at a time. It hopes to grow well, with integrity, tenderness, and lasting purpose.
